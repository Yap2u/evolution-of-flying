Writing
Imagine it’s the year 2050. Flying cars are now a common form of transport. Write a short paragraph describing what a typical day looks like using a flying car. Include ideas about how it changes your daily routine, city life, or the environment.
Tips for Writing:
  • Start with a sentence that introduces the future setting (e.g., “It’s the year 2050, and flying cars are everywhere.”)
  • Use the present or future tense to describe your day.
  • Include at least one advantage of flying cars (e.g., faster travel, less pollution).
  • Try to use at least two vocabulary words from the previous activity.
  • Finish with a thought or question about the future of transport.
